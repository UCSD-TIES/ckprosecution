Marine Related Prosections in California

Historically, prosecutions in San Diego are low compared to other beach communities, such as Orange Counties. Those who do get prosecuted in San Diego are usually given a fine, merely a slap-on-the-wrist, while consequences in Orange County involve jail time. Coastkeeper needs a tool that allows them to easily record, compare, and visualize data from marine-related prosecutions throughout California.
